With a top speed of 380 km/h (236 mph) and the ability to go from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.4 seconds, it's an absolute beast on the road. But it's not all about speed; the Divo excels in handling too. The aggressive lines and striking aesthetics make it a head-turner, while the luxurious interior offers a blend of high-tech functionality and opulence. For me, the Bugatti Divo represents the perfect fusion of beauty and power, making it my favorite hypercar.
